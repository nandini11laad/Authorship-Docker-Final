Authorship Identification

The rapid development and proliferation of internet technologies caused online messages to be shared largely on web -based channels.The anonymous nature of online message distribution makes the identity tracing a critical problem.Hence we developed a framework for authorship identification of online messages to address the identity-tracing problem.
